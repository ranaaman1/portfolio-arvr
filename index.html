Professional Overview

My expertise lies in converting complex spaces—campuses, defence systems, and architecture—into reliable virtual experiences using tools like Twinmotion, Unreal Engine, Unity, and Blender. I focus on performance-driven environment design, lighting and material optimization, and intuitive interaction systems that feel natural in VR.

My professional journey includes leading multi-semester projects, winning expo and AI fest awards, and presenting work to academic and industry panels.
